What we do

Empower you with creative private credit solutions, global reach, and actionable world-class advice

Zero N is a US based global investment advisory firm that combines the expansive reach of international deal-making and trade, with the power of radical innovation, to enable the creation, and accelerated growth, of high-impact ventures in areas vital to global stability, national interest, and sustainability. 

This combination has the advantage of simultaneously lowering the risk for all parties involved, while boosting performance and long-term business sustainability. 

Our deep experience in both technology and international business sets us apart from fair-weather opportunistic players in the field. 

 

Our innovative business model includes a suite of best-in-class advisory services, an early stage venture capital arm, and a bespoke private credit & asset monetization solutions.  This model makes us uniquely positioned to partner with organizations and individuals to chart a bold and sustainable path towards a future that meets their short term needs, and long-term objectives in an increasingly volatile global landscape.

Our expertise span different disciplines, sectors,  geographies, and our team is prepared to work with our clients from conception & planning to financing & execution. Our approach combines the mutually beneficial tradition of international trade, the power of innovation, and a first-grade network of global partners to turbo-charge your plans, and unlock the full potential of your assets.
