MSD Capital, L.P. Established in 1998, exclusively manages the assets of Michael S. Dell and his family. MSD Capital utilizes a multi-disciplinary investment strategy focused on maximizing long-term capital appreciation by making investments across the globe in the equities of public and private companies, credit, real estate and other asset classes and securities.

MSD Capital's team operates from the firm's offices in New York and West Palm Beach.
